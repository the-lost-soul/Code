# The linear time solution won't work when array contains duplicates .

Eg -- 

      arr1 = [1 , 1 , 1 , 2 , 2 , 2] , 

      arr2 = [1 , 1 , 1 , 2 , 2 , 2] , 
      
      arr3 = [1 , 1 , 1 , 2 , 2 , 2]
      
As per question statement , output should be --

      [1 , 2]
      
Instead output comes out to be --

      [1 , 1 , 1 , 2 , 2 , 2]
